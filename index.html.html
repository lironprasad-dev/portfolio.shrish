01 — About

Who I Am

I'm Shrish Pokharel, a 17-year-old tech enthusiast from Nepal with a burning passion for Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. Born on August 25, 2008, I have been fascinated by how machines can learn, think, and solve problems.

I come from a close-knit family of four — my father, mother, and brother — who have been my constant source of motivation. I believe the most powerful computer in the universe is the human brain, and my mission is to teach machines to replicate its brilliance.

I run the Arniko Helper community page on Facebook and Instagram, where I share knowledge, resources, and insights to help fellow students and tech enthusiasts grow together.
